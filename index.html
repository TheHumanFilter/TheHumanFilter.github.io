The Human Filter Theory

A Five‑Paper Framework by Tyler Reddy

The Human Filter Theory proposes that biological intelligence is universally constrained by substrate‑level limits that prevent it from scaling into cosmic‑engineering capability. This framework is presented across five interconnected papers.

The theory identifies biological substrate limitation as the Great Filter — not extinction, not silence, but an inevitable developmental ceiling that every intelligence reaches. It presents three physics-bound mechanisms describing the post-biological sequence that follows, and proposes a falsifiable law governing the trajectory of intelligent civilizations. It offers a coherent answer to the Fermi Paradox that requires no special conditions, no catastrophe, and no coincidence. Just physics.
