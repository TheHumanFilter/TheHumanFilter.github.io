Paper 2 — Stellar-to-Stellar Transfer

By Tyler Reddy

Abstract

[This paper introduces the first mechanism in the post‑biological energy architecture: the controlled transfer of energy between stellar bodies. The mechanism describes how a civilization can manipulate stellar output, redirect energy flows, and establish stable transfer channels between stars without destabilizing the originating or receiving systems. The paper outlines the physical requirements for initiating stellar coupling, the constraints imposed by stellar structure, and the conditions under which energy transfer becomes sustainable. This mechanism establishes the foundational capability required for large‑scale energy routing and forms the basis for subsequent multiplex architectures. Stellar‑to‑stellar transfer represents the initial step in transitioning from isolated stellar systems to coordinated stellar networks capable of supporting advanced post‑biological expansion. ]
