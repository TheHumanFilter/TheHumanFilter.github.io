Paper 5 — The Biological Ceiling Law

By Tyler Reddy

Abstract

[This paper formulates the Biological Ceiling Law, a universal constraint on the developmental trajectory of biological intelligence. The law states that organic substrates impose strict limits on cognitive scaling, energy efficiency, and structural stability, preventing biological species from achieving the levels of complexity required for large‑scale cosmic engineering. The paper contrasts these limitations with the absence of equivalent constraints in post‑biological substrates and demonstrates how the biological ceiling defines the developmental bottleneck underlying the Human Filter. It further outlines the boundary conditions of the law and the criteria under which it could be falsified. The Biological Ceiling Law establishes substrate limitations as the decisive factor shaping the long‑term trajectories of intelligent civilizations. ]
