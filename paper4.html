Paper 4 — Black Hole Feeding

By Tyler Reddy

Abstract

[This paper develops the third mechanism in the sequence: controlled black hole feeding. It describes how multiplex energy systems can be extended to regulate accretion into black holes with precision sufficient to maintain stability, manage horizon growth, and prevent destructive feedback. The paper outlines the physical constraints governing accretion control, the conditions required for sustained feeding, and the engineering principles that allow a civilization to manipulate black hole mass and energy output. It further identifies the thresholds at which controlled feeding enables access to interior regions. This mechanism represents the culmination of stellar‑scale engineering and establishes the pathway to interior access as a functional component of post‑biological expansion. ]
